About KK University

KK University is a premier institution inspired by global leaders like MIT, dedicated to technology-driven education, entrepreneurship, and research excellence. Located in Pune, Maharashtra, our vibrant campus fosters innovation, industry partnerships, and holistic development. We empower students to tackle real-world challenges and become leaders in their fields.

Founded with a vision to bridge academia and industry, we offer state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a dynamic learning environment.
